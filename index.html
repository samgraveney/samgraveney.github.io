Home Page

I'm Sam. I like to write. I write short stories and novels, with the odd blog and review as well.

My short stories are here, on this Medium. Some were written for competitions, some for writing courses, some just for fun (and one to test a typewriter). I've been published in a few places, and have recently shortlisted for the Oxford Flash Fiction competition.

I am currently seeking representation for my fourth novel, Broken Teeth, a lean thriller set in the Alps. You can read the opening here.

I've self-published my first three novels, the Henry Lapin Mission, spy thrillers set in the tense and fervid days of the early Cold War. They're pulpy and earnest, and I'm proud of them. They are also free.
